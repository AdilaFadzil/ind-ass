EXPERIENCE

The most memorable experience I will ever forget was when I took the covid 19 vaccine at the age of 20 which is last year. I took the Pfizer vaccine at Dewan Tun Chancellor Multimedia University in Cyberjaya. Although for some people it is just normal, but for me, it is a very meaningful experience because it happens once in a lifetime. As we know, Covid 19 is spreading and to protect themselves, Malaysians must work together to prevent it by getting vaccinated. Although many still have doubts about the validity of the vaccine, I still dared to take the vaccine and now I have completed 2 doses of vaccine. An unforgettable experience in this memory will always be in my memory.
